--- layout: single title: About Me permalink: /about/ author_profile: true classes: wide --- I'm Yao Ma (马遥), a quantum researcher and engineer. I am currently a Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the National University of Singapore (NUS). Prior to joining NUS, I served as a Senior Research Associate in the Quantum Communication and Cryptography team at JPMorganChase in Singapore. I received my Ph.D. from LIP6, Sorbonne University, France, under the supervision of Prof. Elham Kashefi, Dr. Myrto Arapinis, and Dr. Marc Kaplan. Before my doctoral studies, I worked as a Hardware Engineer at Veriqloud, France, a start-up specializing in quantum communication networks and cybersecurity solutions. From where I start my journey in quantum research. My research interests include quantum communication, quantum cryptography, quantum networks, and quantum information theory. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical applications in quantum technologies.
